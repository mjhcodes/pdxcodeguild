Reimbursement Landscape Assessment and Strategy Development

Understanding the current and potential coding, coverage and payment landscape is key to developing a realistic and achievable strategy and tactical plan. We will show you the way.

Coding / Payment Applications

We help our clients secure CPT, HCPCS and ICD-10 codes as well as transitional pass-through status and new technology add-on payments (NTAP).

Payer Coverage / Healthcare Policy Monitoring and Reporting

We regularly monitor active and future payer coverage policies to ensure our clients are up to date on the coverage status of their products and procedures. We also develop healthcare industry policy reports.

Patient / Provider Support Services

We help our clients identify an appropriate level of customer/patient support needed for a successful product launch, can assist in the selection of service vendor(s), oversee program design and development phases, as well as manage and audit post-launch.

Customer / Patient / Payer Marketing Resources

Effectively communicating to your customers is crucial. We create best-in-class billing and coding guides, informational slide decks, leave behinds and sales aids, and other marketing materials aimed to optimize information dissemination in a clear and concise manner.

Reimbursement and Market Access Training

About Directive Strategy Group

Healthcare reimbursement and market access has become the most important variable in the success of emerging healthcare treatments and procedures. Without it, even the best products will fall flat in today’s market.

Directive Strategy Group provides comprehensive reimbursement consulting services to pharma, medical device and molecular diagnostic companies. We work with companies from early on in the pre-commercialization stage, all the way through the product's life cycle. Our services center on understanding the current and potential reimbursement landscapes, which enables us to provide strategic direction and tactical planning to help our clients reach their objectives.
